Service questions

Clear answers before you reach out.

How long do diagnostics take?

Most diagnostics are completed within 24 hours. We run a comprehensive check on hardware, software, and performance metrics to identify the root cause. If your device requires deeper testing, we'll notify you immediately with a clear timeline.

Do you use original parts?

Yes. We prioritize OEM and certified components for all repairs. Whether it's a motherboard, screen, or power supply, we source directly from trusted manufacturers to ensure reliability and warranty coverage on every replacement.

Can you help with data concerns?

Absolutely. We offer secure data backup and recovery services before any repair work begins. Your files, documents, and media are handled with strict confidentiality protocols, and we can transfer everything to a new drive if needed.

Do you support gaming PCs and office devices?

We service both. From high-performance gaming rigs with custom cooling to enterprise workstations and standard office laptops, our technicians are trained across all platforms—Windows, macOS, and Linux—to deliver expert computer repair services for any setup.
